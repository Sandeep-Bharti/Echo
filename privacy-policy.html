Privacy Policy

Echo Journal — Last updated: February 2026

1. Who We Are

Echo Journal ("we", "our", "us") is a private voice journaling app. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is protected. For any privacy questions, contact us at privacy@echojournal.app.

2. Data We Collect

  • Voice recordings — Stored locally on your device. Only uploaded to our cloud if you purchase and enable Cloud Backup.
  • Transcripts & AI insights — Generated from your recordings. Stored locally; synced to cloud only when Cloud Backup is active.
  • Email address — Collected only if you create an account. Used for authentication and account recovery.
  • Purchase history — Managed by RevenueCat and your device's app store (Apple/Google). We never see your payment card details.
  • Usage analytics — We do not collect analytics or track app usage beyond what RevenueCat provides for subscription management.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide transcription and AI insight features on your journal entries.
  • To sync your journal to the cloud (only when you explicitly enable Cloud Backup).
  • To manage your subscription and restore purchases across devices.
  • To send reminders you configure (notification content stays on-device).

4. Third-Party Services

Echo Journal uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — Cloud database and authentication for account and Cloud Backup features. Privacy Policy
  • AssemblyAI — Audio transcription. Your audio is sent to AssemblyAI's servers for processing when you request transcription. Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic (Claude) — AI insight generation. Your transcript text is sent to Anthropic's API when you request AI insights. Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription and in-app purchase management. Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Workers — Proxy server that routes requests to AssemblyAI and Anthropic. No data is stored on Cloudflare Workers. Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Voice recordings and journal data are stored locally on your device by default.
  • Cloud Backup data is stored in Supabase with Row-Level Security — only you can access your data.
  • Audio files in cloud storage are stored in a private bucket accessible only by your authenticated account.
  • We use HTTPS for all data in transit.

6. Data Retention

  • Local data — Retained until you delete entries or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud data — Retained as long as your account exists. Deleting your account removes all cloud data within 30 days.
  • Transcription audio — AssemblyAI deletes uploaded audio files within 24 hours of processing, per their data retention policy.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Export your journal data (available in the app).
  • Delete your account and all associated cloud data.
  • Opt out of Cloud Backup by not enabling or cancelling the Cloud Backup subscription.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@echojournal.app.

8. Children's Privacy

Echo Journal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email us at privacy@echojournal.app.
